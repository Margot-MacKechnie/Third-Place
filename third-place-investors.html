For Investors

Join us in rebuilding community, one gathering space at a time

Third Place represents a unique investment opportunity at the intersection of social impact and sustainable business. We're addressing the loneliness epidemic through a proven business model—combining independent bookstore, specialty café, and plant shop retail with intentional community-building design.

Below you'll find comprehensive documentation of our business model, market analysis, financial projections, and strategic approach. We welcome conversations with mission-aligned investors who share our vision for creating spaces where community naturally thrives.

The Three Pillars

Each element serves a purpose—together, they create community

Read

Our independent bookstore celebrates the power of stories to connect us. We curate selections that reflect our community—highlighting local authors, amplifying diverse voices, and creating space for book clubs and author events that spark meaningful conversations.

What You'll Find:

  • Carefully curated fiction and non-fiction
  • Local and independent author showcases
  • Community book club meetings
  • Author readings and signings
  • Cozy reading nooks throughout the space

Sip

Coffee and conversation go hand in hand. Our café offers specialty coffee, fresh-baked pastries, and comfort food made from scratch. It's not just fuel—it's the excuse to stay, the reason to linger, and the backdrop for connections to form naturally.

What You'll Find:

  • Artisan coffee and specialty drinks
  • Fresh pastries and baked goods daily
  • Seasonal soups, sandwiches, and comfort food
  • Communal tables for conversation
  • Free WiFi for remote work and study

Grow

Plants bring life and peace to any space. Our plant shop offers houseplants for every skill level, gardening supplies, and workshops on sustainable urban gardening. Whether you're a seasoned plant parent or just starting, we help you grow—literally and figuratively.

What You'll Find:

  • Houseplants for all experience levels
  • Gardening tools and sustainable supplies
  • Plant care workshops and classes
  • Expert advice and plant consultations
  • A thriving community of plant lovers

A New Kind of Gathering Space

Third Place reimagines the community gathering space by bringing together an independent bookstore, specialty café, and plant shop under one roof. More than just a retail space, we're creating an environment where people naturally connect, discover, and belong—addressing the loneliness epidemic through thoughtfully designed social infrastructure.
